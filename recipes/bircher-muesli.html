Bircher Muesli

Description:

The Bircher Muesli is a strong breakfast option, being a customizable combination of oats, nuts, seeds, honey, fruit, yogurt, milk, and more...

Albeit requiring preparation and needing to be set out for refrigeration overnight, you'd be hard-pressed trying to find a better morning meal!

Pronounced (MEWS-lee), this baby can fit so much satisfaction in it you'll be guaranteed full for hours!

Ingredients:

To start out, here are the original ingredients of Dr. Bircher's Muesli - but don't forget about the flexibility of this recipe - mix it up to your heart's content!
  • 1 tablespoon rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 large apple, unpeeled and grated
  • chopped nuts, hazelnut, almond, or walnut

Steps:

  1. Add everything but the nuts into the bowl.
  2. Let it sit in the refrigerator, preferrably overnight (but at the very least for one hour).
  3. Mix in the nuts as a topping.
  4. If the mixture is too thick, add a bit of water, milk, juice, or anything wet. That's all there is to it!

Here are a few extra suggestions to swap in or add on!

  • Some yogurt or any other milk variation in place of the condensed milk.
  • Dried fruit in place of (or alongside) the nuts.
  • Fresh berries in place of (or alongside) the apple.
  • Orange juice, apple juice, or even nothing in place of the lemon juice.
  • A drizzle of honey, maple syrup, brown sugar, cinnamon, etc.
  • Sprinkle on some seeds, sunflower, chia, etc.
